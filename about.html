About

We're an online shop selling men and woman's rain jackets. They are designed for a range of different outdoor activities that enriches people's lives.

The jackets are meant for men and woman aged 30 to 50 whose interests are: being outdoors, hiking, exploring, skiing, camping, canoeing.

The jackets are mid-range in price and emphasize durability and being suitable for a range of different weather types ensuring customers are comfortable whatever adventure they go on.
